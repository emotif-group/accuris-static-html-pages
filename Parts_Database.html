Case Studies

Texas Instruments Enhances Customer Service by Expanding Cross References from 100K to 2M

Building Design & Construction Electronics

One of the world’s premier semiconductor companies increased the number of cross references in its parts database from 100,000 to nearly 2 million. In doing so, the company boosted results of its parts with the lowest searches by 52% and helped its customers find alternatives for obsolete parts so they can avoid the need to redesign products.

IEC Electronics defuses supply chain risks and reduces RFP turnaround from 14 to 3 days

Electronics Manufacturing

As a leader in electronic manufacturing services, this US-based company streamlined work processes across six departments, reduced turnaround on RFP deliverables from as much as two weeks to 72 hours, enabled customers to plan for product end-of-life issues and achieved strong ROI by enabling one staffer to do the work of five employees.

Scott Wilson

View Profile

Scott has over 30 years of experience developing business information services to support critical engineering, compliance, and supply chain needs. He leads Accuris Content Services specializing in developing content, tools and processes to address product material compliance such as RoHS, REACH, SCIP; conflict minerals compliance; export control; and supply chain issues such as obsolescence, constrained supply, risk, and pricing. Prior to Accuris, Scott worked in technical positions at General Instrument, Tektronix, Advantest and i2 Technologies. Scott has a bachelor’s degree in Physics and Computer Science from the State University of New York at Oswego. He speaks English and has a basic knowledge of French and German.

Ross Beiler

View Profile

Ross has over 30 years of experience managing decision support tools used by electrical design engineers, component engineers, supply chain, compliance, and sustainment professionals. The tools support solutions for engineering, supply chain, and procurement users for numerous customers in the Hi-Tech, Telecommunications, Aerospace and Defense, Automotive, Medical, Manufacturing, and Process industries. His skills include Agile product owner, product management, project management and solution architecture of parts management systems, enterprise information systems, decision support systems, component and supplier management, component obsolescence, component environmental compliance, strategic sourcing, and workflow process automation.
